What Defines Each Archetype

Archetype Key Characteristics Best For

Archetype 01

Dark Luxury

 Deep blacks, charcoal tones, velvet headboards, custom millwork, gold hardware. The penthouse aesthetic. High-rise apartments, executive suites, those who value opulence and sophistication.

Archetype 02

Cozy Masculine

 Warm earth tones, plush textiles, dim Edison bulbs. Mountain cabin meets city apartment. Loft spaces, studio apartments, those seeking comfort and a lived-in refuge.

Archetype 03

Minimalist Precision

 Every object earns its place. No decorative clutter. Neutral palette, clean lines, disciplined design. Small spaces, home offices, those who value clarity and intentional living.

Archetype 04

Dark Academia

 Filled bookshelves, antique desk lamps, chesterfield sofas. Deep greens and browns for thinkers. Home libraries, studies, scholars and those who read, study, and think deeply.
